Homemade Watermelon–Mango–Mint–Lemon Syrup
Ingredients
120 ml watermelon syrup
120 ml mango syrup
1 handful fresh mint leaves (10–15 leaves)
60 ml fresh lemon juice
60–120 ml water
Optional: 1–2 tbsp sugar
Instructions
1
Lightly crush the mint leaves to release their oils.
2
In a saucepan, combine watermelon syrup, mango syrup, and water. Heat gently — do not boil — for 3–5 minutes.
3
Add mint leaves and steep on low heat for 5 minutes. Turn off heat and let sit covered for 10–15 minutes.
4
Strain out the mint leaves. Stir in fresh lemon juice.
5
Taste and adjust sweetness or acidity if needed. Cool completely, then bottle and refrigerate. Keeps about 1 week.
Great for
Mix with sparkling water · Add to cocktails or mocktails · Drizzle over fruit or desserts · Mix into iced tea
